Computer & Information Research Scientist

A computer and information research scientist is also called a computer specialist, computer scientist, and research scientist.

What They Do

Computer and information research scientists condict research into the basics of computer and information science. They look at this field of science, not as researchers but as theorists, designers, and inventors. Computer scientists aslo develop sloutions to problems found in the computer hardware and software field.

What to Expect on the Job

You should expect to analyze problems and develop sloutions to them using computer hardware and software. Applying your theoretical expertise and innovation to create or apply new technology is also something that will be requested of you. The job also relies on your ability to assign or schedule tasks to meet work priorties and goals.

Knowledge Needed

You should have knowledge about computers, electronics, product and service development, arithmetic, algebra, gemotry, calculus, statistics, and business managment. You should be fluent and literate in the english langauge.

Skills

The basic skills you will need on the job are beling able to listen to others, not interrupting others when they are speaking, asking good questions, and problem sovling skills. Some more indepth people and technology systems skills you may need are figuring out how computer systems work and how changes to the system will affect it, and being able to think about the pros and cons of multiple sloutions and picking the best one.

Abilities

You need basic math ablilites like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and being able to choose the righ type of math to solve a problem. The ability to make generak rules or come up with the answers from lots of detailed information and using those rules to solve problems are also valuable abilities to have on the job. You should also be able to communicate by speaking up and listen and understand what people say to you.

Personality and O*NET Intrest Profile

People who are Realistic, Investigative, and Conventional, according to the O*NET Intrest Profiler, should be computer scientists. Other personality traits that you need on this job are analytical thinking, cooperation, attention to detail, independence, effort/drive, and initiative.

Technology Used

Some technology you can exprect to use on the job are Apache Kafka, Oracle Java 2 Platform Enterprise Edition J2EE, Mocrosoft Powerpoint, SAS, and Mathworks MATLAB.

Education

The education needed for this job is usually a bachelor's or doctorate degree in computer science.

Job Outlook

The job outlook on this career is very bright and there are likley to be many new job opportunities in the future.

Salary

The median salary for computer information and research scientists is around $136,620, the lower 10% earn around $78,190, and the top 10% of workers earn around $232,010.
